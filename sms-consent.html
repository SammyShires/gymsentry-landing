How GymSentry Works

GymSentry is a member retention platform for gyms and martial arts schools. Your gym coach has enrolled your facility in our weekly check-in system to help track member progress and engagement.

What Messages You'll Receive

  • Members: 1 SMS per week with a link to complete your 30-second check-in survey
  • Coaches: 2-3 SMS per week with action alerts for at-risk members
  • Follow-ups: Occasional messages from your coach based on your check-in responses

SMS Consent & Disclosures

By participating in GymSentry, you consent to receive:

  • Automated SMS messages from GymSentry on behalf of your gym
  • Message frequency: Approximately 1-4 messages per week
  • Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan

Opt-Out Instructions:

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately
  • Reply HELP to get customer support information

Support:

  • Email: sammy@gymsentry.com
  • Website: gymsentry.com
  • SMS Support Number: (615) 235-5357

How You're Enrolled

  1. Your gym owner/coach signs up for the GymSentry pilot program
  2. Coach provides member contact information during onboarding
  3. You receive an initial welcome SMS with opt-out instructions
  4. You receive weekly check-in reminders via SMS

Your Privacy & Data Rights

Your phone number and check-in data are stored securely and only used for:

  • Sending weekly check-in reminders
  • Alerting your coach if you're at risk of disengagement
  • Improving your gym experience

We will NEVER:

  • Sell your phone number to third parties
  • Send marketing messages unrelated to your gym
  • Share your data with other gyms or facilities

Supported Keywords

STOP Keywords: STOP, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE

Response: "You've been unsubscribed from GymSentry. You won't receive further messages. Reply START to resubscribe."

HELP Keywords: HELP, INFO, SUPPORT

Response: "GymSentry weekly check-ins. Msg frequency varies. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel. Help: sammy@gymsentry.com"
